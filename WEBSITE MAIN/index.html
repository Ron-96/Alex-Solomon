About

Alex Solomon is a South African artist whose distinctive voice, raw lyricism, and unwavering dedication to her craft have made her a standout emerging talent on both the South African and international music scenes.
Known for her unique blend of pop, pop-rock, and indie, Alex’s music effortlessly combines deep introspection with bold, unapologetic flair, crafting songs that are both emotionally resonant and irresistibly captivating.
With every track, she draws listeners in on a profound level, keeping them on the edge of their seats and eager for what’s next.
